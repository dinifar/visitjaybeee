Muafakat Johor Bus Routes

Overall Easy Diagram at a glance. (Click to enlarge)

Free bus services for Malaysian only.

With the introduction of Bas Muafakat Johor in April 2016, bus commuters in Johor Bahru will see new connections or reintroduction of bus services in some areas without bus services. In this first phase of introduction, 30 buses will be wrapped with the new Bas Muafakat Johor branding and 15 bus routes will be rolled out.

JB Di Hatiku free bus service JB1 by Majlis Bandaraya Johor Bahru (MBJB) was rebranded and renumbered as Bas Muafakat Johor bus route P101.

The expansion of this initiative was rolled out in April 2017, with an additional 11 new bus routes in Johor Bahru and a total of 20 new bus routes in Johor towns such as Muar, Tangkak, Yong Peng, Segamat, Simpang Renggam, Kluang, Batu Pahat, Pontian, Mersing and Kota Tinggi.
